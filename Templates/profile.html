Projects

Circle Of Excellence

I have designed and developed two Power Apps as part of my project. The first app enables individuals within the organization to submit nominations for any member they deem worthy. The second app, exclusive to administrators, facilitates the review of all nominations and empowers them to either approve or reject the submissions. To streamline the business process, I have integrated a Power Automate flow, which allows the approved data to be exported effortlessly in both Excel and PDF formats. This integration significantly enhances the efficiency of the business operations, expediting the nomination and approval processes. Overall, these Power Apps and the integrated Power Automate flow serve as a valuable asset in optimizing and accelerating the organization's nomination and approval procedures.
